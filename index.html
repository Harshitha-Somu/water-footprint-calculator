About This Website

What is this?
A tool to show water used in food production
Why needed?
To reduce hidden agricultural water usage
Main focus
Crop-wise water footprint awareness
Core goal
Promote sustainable food & farming choices
Impact
Lower water stress and better crop planning

How to Use This Website

Step 1
Select User or Farmer mode from the header
Step 2
Enter the crop or food item you want to check
Step 3
(Farmer mode) Select suitable soil and climate
Step 4
System matches data from the dataset and shows water footprint
Step 5
Compare alternatives to choose crops with lower water usage
